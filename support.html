Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install a widget?

On your Home Screen, tap and hold an empty area until the icons jiggle. Tap the "+" button and search for "Percent Remaining" to add the widget.

How do I adjust my life expectancy settings?

Open the app, tap on the "Me" tab, and update your details such as birthdate and lifestyle estimates.

How are notifications handled?

Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and never sent through external servers.
